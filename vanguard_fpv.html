Educational Curriculum

Tier 1: Fundamentals

The Proving Ground. Master the "Acro" feel, throttle control, and coordinated turns in an open environment before attempting complex maneuvers.

Tier 2: Navigation

Nap-of-the-Earth flying under 1 meter altitude, building entry through small windows, and maintaining Line of Sight (LOS) in ruined suburban environments.

Tier 3: Kinetic Ops

Accounting for weight and momentum. Execute kamikaze runs on moving targets (40km/h) and calculate intercept trajectories with heavy payloads.

Tier 4: EW & Stress

Operate under duress with simulated Jamming (latency/signal flicker), battery voltage sag, and audio cues of nearby combat.
