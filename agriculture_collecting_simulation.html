Digitizing the Field

This project aims to develop a VR training system that transports agricultural workforce training into a digital environment. Specifically targeting the strawberry picking process, it enables workers to learn correct picking techniques and improve their application skills in a realistic virtual setting.

Correct Technique

Mastering fruit selection and handling without damaging the crop.

Time Management

Optimizing picking speed and efficiency in a timed environment.

Unique Approach

Simulating Precision

What sets this project apart is its focus on agriculture-specific manual processes rather than generic skills. We simulate the exact picking motion using advanced hand tracking, allowing users to practice the precise harvesting maneuver repeatedly.

Scalable Architecture: This system isn't limited to strawberries. Its flexible structure can be adapted for any crop or agricultural scenario.

Waste Reduction: By training virtually, product waste during the learning phase is eliminated.

Immersive Control

Precision in Your Hands

Picking a strawberry requires more than just a button press. It demands delicacy, the right angle, and precise pressure. Our advanced Hand Tracking system eliminates the need for controllers, allowing users to use their natural hand movements. This 1:1 fidelity ensures that the muscle memory developed in VR translates perfectly to the real field, preventing fruit damage and ensuring the highest quality harvest.
