The Challenge

Zero-Risk
Cryogenic Handling

Delivering liquid nitrogen is a high-stakes operation. One mistake in valve sequencing or pressure management can lead to equipment failure or severe injury. This project digitizes the entire delivery workflow, allowing drivers to practice dangerous procedures without real-world consequences.

Workflow

Standard Operating
Procedures

01

Site Stabilization

Before any transfer begins, drivers must secure the vehicle. We simulate the physical placement of wheel chocks and safety cone deployment to ensure site compliance.

02

Mechanical Assembly

Users interact with heavy industrial equipment using VR controllers. Tightening flanges, checking seals, and using non-sparking tools are simulated with physics-based feedback.

03

Pressure Management

The core transfer process. Drivers must monitor analog gauges and operate valves in the correct sequence to manage the flow of liquid nitrogen through the vaporizer units.

Technical Depth

Simulating
Industrial Physics

This isn't just a 360-degree video; it's a fully interactive simulation. We modeled the physical resistance of rusty valves, the weight of the transfer hoses, and the auditory feedback of gas pressure to create a "training-by-doing" environment.

Haptic Interaction

Fine motor control required for bolt tightening and valve adjustments.

Dynamic Systems

Gauges react in real-time to user input, simulating potential over-pressure scenarios.
